About

Since opening in 2004, LUSHY Hair Studio has become known for calm, refined hair work — built on talent, thoughtful technique, and a team committed to helping you feel naturally confident.

Our approach is guided by intention. Each stylist receives ongoing training from respected educators, ensuring that every cut and color uses modern, gentle techniques suited to your hair. The studio is designed for comfort — soft lighting, open space, and a relaxed atmosphere that supports careful, creative work. We love subtle detail: clean lines, blended color, and shapes that look effortless in everyday life.
